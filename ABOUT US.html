ABOUT US


"Timeless Treasures for the Modern Souls."



Welcome to Mira's Vintage Finds, where each piece tells a story, weaving the timeless elegance of the past with the vibrant spirit of the modern soul. Our journey began from a simple yet profound love for vintage fashion and a belief in the transformative p ower of style. Born from the creative vision of our founder, Mira, our brand is a testament to the idea that old clothes can be reimagined and revitalized into modern treasures. It's about more than just fashion; it's about sharing a piece of history, a moment in time brought back to life with innovation and the right style.

Our Mission and Values: At Mira's Vintage Finds, our mission is to redefine the perception of vintage clothing. We aim to prove that with a touch of creativity, what was once old can be new and fashionable again. We are committed to sustainability, cherishing the past while looking forward to the future, and demonstrating that style is timeless. Our core values revolve around quality, sustainability, and individuality. We believe in providing our customers with unique pieces that not only make them stand out but also contribute to a more sustainable fashion industry.

What We Offer: Our collection ranges from classic jackets, each with its own story and character. We meticulously curate our selection to ensure that every item reflects the beauty and quality of bygone eras, ready to be integrated into the modern wardrobe. Our offerings are not just products; they are solutions to the fast-paced and often wasteful world of fashion, providing an eco-friendly and stylish alternative.

Since our inception, Mira's Vintage Finds has grown from a passionate project into a beloved brand with a loyal following. We've celebrated milestones from our first pop-up shop to launching our online store, each step marking our growth and commitment to spreading the love for vintage fashion. Our team, a small but mighty group, brings diverse expertise and a shared vision to the brand. From sourcing to styling, they're the heartbeat of Mira's Vintage Finds, dedicated to finding those perfect pieces that resonate with our customers.

Our Unique Approach: We pride ourselves on a personal touch, seeing the beauty in imperfections, and bringing a new life to each garment or accessory. Our approach goes beyond simply selling; it's about creating a community of individuals who appreciate the stories behind their style, who value sustainability, and who are bold enough to express themselves through unique, vintage finds.

Join Our Journey: We invite you to explore our collection and become part of the Mira's Vintage Finds family. Whether you're seeking to add a touch of vintage flair to your wardrobe or searching for that one-of-a-kind piece, we're here to help you find your timeless treasure.
